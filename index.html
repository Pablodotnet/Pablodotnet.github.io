About Me

I am Luis Pablo López, software developer from México, graduated in Computer Engineering from Universidad Autónoma de Baja Califonia in Ensenada, Baja California. On the last years of high school I got involved with web development and found my passion. That's what I'm doing at the moment and I love each aspect of my job, I've always said that if you don't love what you are doing, don't do it!

In the course of my career in college, a colleague and I formed a work team which we called Lagartija Web Design, this started as a school project, but a year later, with another team member we decided to start doing freelance projects, but then the opportunity to create Nutriconsultor, be part of the founding team and of the society appeared for us and we took it, so this project was put on hold. Years later, at the end of 2018 we decided to revive this small project now called Lagartija Development, in which we are dedicated to developing web sites and applications, thanks to this project I had the opportunity to learn about project management, dealing with clients, assembling and management of Google Analytics, SEO and a bit of digital marketing and design.
